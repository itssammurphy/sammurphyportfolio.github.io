About Me

G'day!

I'm Sam, a 15 year-old aspiring front-end developer from Melbourne, Australia. This is my website! This is where I'll be showcasing my coding projects and help further develop my skills with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. This website will be undergoing almost constant change as I attempt to tune every minute detail to my liking, so watch this space! To keep up-to-date with my coding projects, join my discord server, a place where like-minded developers can collaborate and share ideas.

I've been coding since 2014. I learnt Python when I was in Year 3 at school, and continued to use Python to solve coding problems on a daily basis. I am currently learning Java, and have previously used C++ and JavaScript. I have a strong interest in IoT and web development, as well as algorithm design.

Beyond coding, I play a lot of sport. In summer, I play cricket and throughout the rest of the year, I always try to slot a round of golf in. I also play video games (probably more than I'm willing to admit), and have recently been fixated on Splitgate and Rocket League.

Back to the matter at hand though, I want to use this website as a place to share my development in coding with people, and share any cool or useful projects that may eventuate from this journey. I also hope that this website can help me network with people whom with I can collaborate with on future projects, and help further expand my skills in a diverse range of programming fields.
